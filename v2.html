FAQ

Questions, answered

How does pricing work?+
We tailor quotes based on your business size, goals, and the services you select. After you submit your plan, we send a custom proposal within 1 business day — no hidden fees, no surprise charges. You approve the scope and price before anything starts.
How do engagements work?+
Your service activates within 24 hours of approval. Monthly engagements renew each month, yearly engagements renew once a year. Manage or cancel any time from your dashboard.
Can I cancel at any time?+
Yes. Monthly engagements cancel before the next billing date, no penalty. Yearly engagements stop future renewals — you keep access for the remaining period. No cancellation fees, no lock-in.
How does Sales Staff Augmentation work?+
We assign a trained sales rep who works under your brand, uses your tools, and follows your process — reporting to you weekly. Onboarding takes 3–5 business days. No employment contracts, no HR overhead.
Do I need existing Odoo to use the CRM services?+
No. We set up a fresh Odoo instance as part of onboarding, or work within your existing deployment. For full Odoo ERP (not just CRM), ask our partner team — an official Odoo Ready Partner.
Can I mix bundles with à la carte services?+
Absolutely. Start with a bundle and add individual services on top. Your quote will reflect the combined scope — full flexibility.
