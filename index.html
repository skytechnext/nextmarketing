Common questions

How do subscriptions work?+
Once you subscribe via the portal, your card is charged immediately and your service activates within 24 hours. Monthly plans renew automatically each month; yearly plans renew once per year. You can manage or cancel at any time from your portal dashboard.
Can I cancel at any time?+
Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled before the next billing date with no penalty. Yearly plans are non-refundable but you keep access for the remaining period. No cancellation fees or lock-in contracts.
Can I mix packages and à la carte services?+
Absolutely. Start with a bundle and add individual services on top using the Build Your Plan calculator. Each subscription is billed as a separate line item — full flexibility to mix and match.
How does Sales Staff Augmentation work?+
We assign a trained sales rep to your business. They use your tools (or we set them up), work under your brand, follow your sales process, and report directly to you weekly. Onboarding takes 3–5 business days. No employment contracts, no HR overheads.
Do I need an existing Odoo instance for the CRM services?+
No. We can set up a fresh Odoo instance as part of onboarding, or work within your existing deployment. If you need a full Odoo ERP implementation (not just CRM), speak to our partner team at TechNext — an official Odoo Ready Partner.
What payment methods are accepted?+
All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through our secure portal. Corporate cards welcome. All transactions are in USD and secured by industry-standard encryption.
