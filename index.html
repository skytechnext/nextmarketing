FAQ

Questions, answered

How do subscriptions work?+
Subscribe via the portal with a credit card. Your service activates within 24 hours. Monthly plans renew each month, yearly plans renew once a year. Manage or cancel any time from your dashboard.
Can I cancel at any time?+
Yes. Monthly plans cancel before the next billing date, no penalty. Yearly plans stop future renewals — you keep access for the remaining period. No cancellation fees, no lock-in.
How does Sales Staff Augmentation work?+
We assign a trained sales rep who works under your brand, uses your tools, and follows your process — reporting to you weekly. Onboarding takes 3–5 business days. No employment contracts, no HR overhead.
Do I need existing Odoo to use the CRM services?+
No. We set up a fresh Odoo instance as part of onboarding, or work within your existing deployment. For full Odoo ERP (not just CRM), ask our partner team — an official Odoo Ready Partner.
What payment methods are accepted?+
All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via our secure portal. Corporate cards welcome. All transactions in USD, industry-standard encryption.
Can I mix bundles with à la carte services?+
Absolutely. Start with a bundle and add individual services on top. Each subscription is a separate line item — full flexibility.
